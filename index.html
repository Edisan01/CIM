Why the retainer?

A hotel mandate takes six months of our calendar. Pitchbooks, flights, notary calls, buyer introductions, data room curation, operator background checks, lease term renegotiation. That time has a cost, and a broker who works for free is a broker who isn't working.

The retainer aligns us. You get a desk that picks up the phone, flies to site, and stays on it until the ink is dry. We get the room to do the job properly — without cutting corners or chasing tyre-kickers.

"I'm a broker. I don't run around for free. I run because the mandate is signed and the buyer is real."

Target yield for all mandated assets: 6–8% net per annum. Below that, we don't list it. Above that, we close it.

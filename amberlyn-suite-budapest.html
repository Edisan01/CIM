The Amberlyn Suite Hotel (Sophie Palace) is a fully operational 83-unit aparthotel in the heart of Budapest's Palace District — one of the city's most rapidly appreciating central neighbourhoods. The building was completed in Q2 2020, received its occupancy authorisation in April 2020, and earned the Golden Novum Design Award (NDA) 2019 for its lobby design.

Each apartment is fully furnished and equipped — from studios of 38 m² to 100 m² duplex units — with full kitchens, marble bathrooms, air-conditioning and premium finishes throughout. 46 underground parking spaces are included in the sale. The building has 8 floors, a lobby bar, fitness area, community space and a panoramic roof terrace with views of the Buda Castle and St. Stephen's Basilica.

Investment Case

Budapest's Palace District —
aparthotel play.

The Amberlyn Suite combines the capital appreciation dynamics of a central Budapest residential asset with the yield profile of a fully operational serviced apartment operation. At €325,000 per unit inclusive of 46 underground parking spaces, the pricing reflects the high-quality fitout and central location rather than a distressed entry.

Budapest's short-term rental and aparthotel market has proven highly resilient. The Palace District benefits from growing tourism demand, six university campuses within walking distance, and the ongoing Blaha Lujza Square regeneration programme — a €6M city investment directly improving the immediate streetscape.

The asset can be operated as a full serviced apartment building, leased to an operator, or sold unit-by-unit to end buyers — offering the new owner multiple exit paths.

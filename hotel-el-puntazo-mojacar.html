Hotel El Puntazo is a fully operational beachfront hotel on the Paseo del Mediterráneo in Mojácar — one of Almería's most recognised and authentically Andalusian coastal towns. With 160 keys, direct beach access and established seasonal occupancy, the asset offers both immediate cash yield and a compelling repositioning story.

The Costa de Almería is increasingly regarded as a value-driven alternative to more saturated destinations such as the Costa del Sol and the Balearic Islands — with lower competitive density, growing Northern European demand, and significant ADR growth headroom. The incoming Vera high-speed rail connection will further strengthen access and drive international demand.

At €136,125 per key, Hotel El Puntazo is priced well below replacement cost for a beachfront asset in Spain — offering a rare combination of immediate operational income and tangible repositioning upside in a destination with genuine supply constraints.

The Costa de Almería has been largely overlooked by institutional capital, creating a window of opportunity before the Vera HSR connection reshapes accessibility. Comparable repositioned assets on the Costa del Sol and Balearics command substantially higher ADRs and yields.

The hotel is fully operational with an established seasonal client base. An investor can acquire, stabilise and upgrade — or acquire and reposition to a 4-star offering — within a single business cycle.

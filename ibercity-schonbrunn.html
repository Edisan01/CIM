Hotel Ibercity Wien Schönbrunn is a fully operational 4-star city hotel in Vienna's 12th district, adjacent to Schönbrunn Palace and just 150 m from the Schönbrunn U4 metro station — 10 minutes to the city centre. The 118-key asset sits on 1,791 m² of freehold land with 6,500 m² GBA.

The hotel is fully leased to Ibersol Group (operator of the Ibercity brand) under a Simple Net Lease until 31 December 2040, with a fixed rent schedule escalating to €980/key/month by 2028 and HICP-indexed thereafter. The lease is backed by a 12-month revolving bank guarantee and a corporate guarantee from Ibersol 93 SL, the Spanish parent company.

Ibercity Wien Schönbrunn is a pure income play. The asset is fully leased to Ibersol Group — a listed Portuguese hospitality group — under a Simple Net Lease with no operator risk transferred to the owner. Rent is fixed, paid monthly in advance, and backed by dual guarantees.

Vienna's hotel market has demonstrated consistent occupancy resilience. The Schönbrunn micro-location benefits from year-round tourism demand, proximity to the UNESCO World Heritage Palace, and direct metro access to the city centre.

The rent schedule provides an automatic yield step-up through 2028, then HICP indexation — structuring in inflation protection from day one of ownership.

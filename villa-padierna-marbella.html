Set on 250 hectares of landscaped estate on the Costa del Sol, Villa Padierna Palace is one of Andalucía's most distinguished addresses. The palazzo-style main building houses 149 rooms and suites — many with private terraces overlooking the golf course or Mediterranean. The asset is currently operated by Anantara (NH Group) under a long-term management agreement, delivering institutional-quality EBITDA with a global luxury brand in place.

Three 18-hole golf courses, the 2,700m² Thermes Marins spa, a private beach club 4 km away, six restaurants, ballroom and MICE facilities, and extensive sports infrastructure make this a rare complete resort offering in Western Europe.

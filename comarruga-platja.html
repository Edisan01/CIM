Hotel Comarruga Platja is a fully operational beachfront hotel located at Av. Palfuriana, 115 in Coma-Ruga — a popular seaside town on the Costa Dorada, part of the Tarragona province. The hotel sits 100 metres from the 7-km Coma-Ruga beach, in one of Catalonia's most visited summer tourist destinations.

The asset is being sold operator-free with full vacant possession — ideal for an owner-operator or a buyer seeking to install their own management team. At €58,000 per key, it represents one of the most attractively priced beach hotel acquisitions on the Spanish Mediterranean coast, with immediate income potential and strategic proximity to two major demand generators: PortAventura World (30 min) and Barcelona (57 min).
